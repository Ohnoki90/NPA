Nigerian Psychology
Association

The Nigerian Psychological Association is the apex professional regulatory body in Nigeria responsible for the development, promotion and application of psychology in the public interest.


The Association coordinates training, research and delivery of psychosocial services in order to ensure quality control and preservation of human dignity and safety. It provides avenues for Nigerian psychologists to interact and share experiences related to their academic activities and professional practice with respect to challenges and best practices.

Our Vision

To develop psychological science through standardized -quality training,research and regulated sustainable service delivery in the national and global contexts

Our Mission

To improve humanity through the effective application of psychological knowledge and skills.
